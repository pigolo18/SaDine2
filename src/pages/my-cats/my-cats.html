My Own Cats

Currently, I own 3 adult cats and 4 kittens. Taking care of them is no easy task but somehow I manage to do it everyday.

Feliciano: The Happy One

I usually just call him Fel. He’s a quiet and calm 5 year old cat. Even when he was still a kitten, he would always be alone, sleeping and ignoring his siblings. And unlike most cats that bite when you let them on the stomach area, he usually just stares at you when you do that to him. He likes to ignore me. He likes to ignore the people around him. He may act like that but he usually misses me whenever I leave the house for a few days to spend my vacation somewhere else.

Bear

She used to have a brother named Snow as my mother really liked the Snowbear mint candies. She is Feliciano and Kitkat’s daughter. She acts like Kitkat but she looks more of her father. She is currently pregnant and is bound to give birth next month.

Kitkat

My feline daughter. As a kitten, she was a very playful and active animal. She is now 3 years old and has toned down her playfulness a little bit. She acts like most common cats, but she is the most affectionate out of all my pets that’s why I love her.

The 4 Kittens

These four little black kittens were born on March 24. Currently, they now know how to run.
